About Gorman Restaurant Group

Overview

Founded in 2026, Gorman Restaurant Group is a restaurant operator and development partner focused on building exceptional dining experiences across Kansas.

The company works in partnership with a Dave's Hot Chicken franchise group to develop and operate locations throughout Kansas, bringing one of the fastest-growing restaurant brands in the country to local communities.

Gorman Restaurant Group is focused on building and operating restaurants that feel local, welcoming, and deeply connected to the communities we serve across Kansas.

Our Vision

We believe great restaurants are built by great teams. Our vision is to build restaurants that deliver unforgettable guest experiences while creating meaningful opportunities for the people who help run them.

As we grow across Kansas, our focus remains the same: exceptional food, genuine hospitality, and developing strong leaders within our restaurants.

Looking Ahead

As we continue to expand the Dave's Hot Chicken footprint across Kansas, Gorman Restaurant Group is focused on long-term growth, strong partnerships, and operational excellence.

Our goal is to build restaurants that become meaningful parts of the communities they serve while creating opportunities for our teams to grow alongside the company.
